Frequently Asked Questions

Lease administration is essentially the management of contracts in the real estate sector. It comprises administrating all the tasks that are part of the firm’s lease portfolio. Effective lease administration software helps in reducing risks by ensuring compliance with lease terms, tracking key dates, and providing insights into portfolio performance. By centralizing lease data, organizations can mitigate risks related to missed obligations, financial discrepancies, and legal issues.

By providing automated alerts for critical lease milestones, such as renewal dates and termination options, lease administration software helps prevent costly oversights. Additionally, it facilitates better reporting and analytics, enabling organizations to make informed decisions and proactively address potential risks.

Moreover, lease administration software can integrate with financial systems to ensure accurate financial reporting and compliance with accounting standards, such as ASC 842 and IFRS 16. This integration reduces the risk of financial misstatements and enhances transparency across the organization.
